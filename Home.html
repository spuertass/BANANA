BANANAS "All the magic that they bring"

Banana, the most versatile fruit in the world

Everyone knows bananas, they are everywhere, and everyone has some in their house, is probably the most versatile fruit in the world, you can do anything with banana. There are sweet things such as: Banana Ice cream, Banana Split, Banana shake, Banana bread, Crepes with Banana. In some places they even use bananas in break fast and lunch like in Ecuador where you can use them to cook: Tigrillo, Bolon de Verde, Empanada de Verde, Patacones, Banana Chips, Plantain Chips and many more.

Types of bananas

There are many types of bananas there is not only the common yellow banana, the one that is called “Cavedish Banana” there is also maqueno, platain, red banana, manzano banana, burro banana, lady finger banana, pisanj raja, blue java banana, and many more all of them pat of the same family of fruits and become from a common ancestor “THE KUK”.

Bananas, more than a fruit

But bananas are not only a diverse, good snack and an extremely versatile fruit, banana is a whole symbol is art, is a meme, minions like bananas, monkeys like bananas, everyone likes bananas. In 2016 there was an auction of the art piece called “Comedian” that is basically a banana pasted with tape to the wall, the piece started as a joke, making fun of the modern art, but it had a meaning, it was a joke of the modern art and millionaires couldn’t not buy it, it was finally sold by $120,000 and then a second piece by $150,000. Even though it had some critics and they said that a seven year old could do it, Maurizio Catelan, the creator of the piece, achieved his goal and showed everyone that anything can be art if you want it to be art. Is not a surprise that bananas are a meme, is a funny fruit at the everyone loves bananas.

So, the next time you see a banana remember: “Minions don’t lie, bananas are amazing” -Sebastian Puertas 5/2/2023.
